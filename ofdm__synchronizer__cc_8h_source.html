31  /*!
32  * \brief OFDM frequency synchronizer and timing estimator
33  * \ingroup inspector
34  *
35  * \details
36  * This block can be inserted downstream to the OFDM parameter
37  * estimation block. It performs a frequency offset estimation and
38  * symbol timing estimation according to [1]. Frequency offsets smaller than the
39  * subcarrier spacing will be corrected to obtain orthogonality of
40  * the carriers. Output is a stream with stream tags at the estimated
41  * OFDM symbol beginnings (PMT symbol "symbol").
42  *
43  * Connect output of OFDM parameter estimator to message port and
44  * signal itself to the stream input.
45  *
46  * [1] J. J. van de Beek, M. Sandell, and P. O. Borjesson,
47  * “ML estimation of time and frequency offset in OFDM systems,”
48  * IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing, vol. 45, no. 7, pp.
49  * 1800–1805, 1997. [Online]. Available: http://pure.ltu.se/portal/files/1612155/Article.pdf.
50  */
